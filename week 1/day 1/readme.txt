
What is CRM?
CRM means Customer Relationship Management. Salesforce provides a CRM platformused by businesses to manage interactions with customers and improve relationships withthem. Customer 360 is an AI-powered CRM platform that creates a single, shared view of acustomer by integrating data across sales, service, marketing, commerce, and IT teams inan organisations.

Why companies use Salesforce?
Companies use Salesforce because it helps them manage customers, sales, support and bussiness operations efficiently in one platform.
Salesforce solve the problem of customer relationship management and inefficiency in customer data.
Salesforce works on a cloud based software that gives companies a complete view of their customers so they can build a deeper relationship with customers.
Sales force also helps the different teams( sales , services , IT , marketing , commerce) towork on shared customer data. -It provides a single shared view of the customer data to all the different teams.

Standard Objects:
Account Account refers to an individual or organisation that we want to do bussiness.used to store information about the bussiness or customers.
Contact Contacts are the people belong to a certain organisation that we want to keep track off.A qualified lead can become a contact. Stores information about the individual people associated with an organisation.
Oppurtunity Represents Qualified deals which we will do with organisations. it represents a potential sales deal or bussiness deal. Oppurtunity tab contains several stages to close a deal.
